Velaiilla Pattadhari-fame Hrishikesh-starrer film 'Unarvugal Thodarkathai' directed by Balu Sharma is finally gearing up for release on March 8, 2024. With the romantic film's release around the corner, the film has been cleared by the Censor Board with an U/A certificate.
The film has Miss India Tamil Nadu 2017 Sherlin Seth playing the female lead.
Hrishikesh will be seen as Karthik, a character who works in a wealth management start-up while Sherlin appears as a business development manager in a corporate firm. How they carry their relationship with an exciting past is the main plot.
