Vintage bike and car owners face issues, want govt to resolve them

Mar 10, 2024, 1:00 IST
0
Vintage car and bike owners in Hyderabad are facing difficulties in re-registering their vehicles due to high taxes and absence of a specific policy
Vintage car and bike owners in Hyderabad, many of whom are retired individuals are facing issues in getting their cars re-registered. Right from high taxes applicable on the vintage cars as per policy applicable on re-registration of regular cars, in the absence of a vintage vehicles policy to having norms that mandate having a seat belt, sale invoices, and pollution control certificates, these rules are coming in the way of getting them re-registered.Staff in RTA office are also not trained in dealing with these special class of vehicles and turn away owners showing them the checklist that is applicable for regular vehicles Vintage car owners want a separate counter in the RTA to deal with their issues and separate norms to be applicable to themTelangana has not adopted the central Vintage Cars Policy without which these invaluable vehicles are being treated as any other vehiclesVideos by: Ramoorthy PVideo Editor: Ch Sushil Rao
