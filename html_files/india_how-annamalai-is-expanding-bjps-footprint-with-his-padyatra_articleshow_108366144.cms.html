One yatra, as big as Rahul's, that could reshape BJP in South

  • Makarand R Paranjape
  • TIMESOFINDIA.COMUpdated: Mar 10, 2024, 20:22 IST IST

K Annamalai, BJP's rising star in the South and a former IPS officer, has traversed close to 10,000km during his walkathon in a bid to transform the BJP into a force to reckon with in the land dominated by Dravidian parties

When it comes to ‘yatra politics’, a disproportionate amount of attention has gone to Rahul Gandhi. Most of it, unfortunately, negative. But, arguably, the really game-changing yatra happened in the deep South, far away from the national capital’s political action. I’m speaking of Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) Tamil Nadu president K Annamalai’s En Mann En Makkal (My Land, My People) yatra, which culminated in a grand finale on February 27.
Prime Minister Narendra Modi himself graced the occasion. He was seen approvingly patting Annamalai’s back several times. The clip went viral on social media. A popular handle on X remarked, “One Pat on Back from .@narendramodi is what anyone dreams of! @annamalai_k Ji getting so many!! But he deserves!!”
