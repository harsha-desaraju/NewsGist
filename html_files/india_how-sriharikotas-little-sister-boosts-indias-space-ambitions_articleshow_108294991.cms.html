Explained: How India’s second launchpad offers a straight route to space

  • U Tejonmayam
  • TNNUpdated: Mar 8, 2024, 10:46 IST IST

Kulasekarapattinam in Tamil Nadu will host the country’s second launchpad for rockets, boosting India's space ambitions with the promise of a supportive ecosystem


The latest launchpad for India’s expansive space goals is coming up close to the southern tip of Tamil Nadu, not very far from Kanniyakumari. Prime Minister Narendra Modi laid the foundation stone for Kulasekarapattinam spaceport on February 28 and the facility is expected to start operations in two years.
